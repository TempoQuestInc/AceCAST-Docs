A quick overview of the capabilities of WSV3¶

Noteworthy features include but are not limited to:

  • Near Live Duel-Pol and Terminal radars

  • Satellite imagery

  • Severe Weather

  • METAR

  • Surface Analysis

  • Lightning detection

  • Own data (grib2)

  • Model data

  • Tropical Weather

  • Winter Weather

  • TONS of customizations
